# Algebra

## Linear Equations

$$
y = ax + b
$$

### Solution

$$
x = \frac{-b}{a}
$$

## Quadratic Equations

$$
y = ax^2 + bx + c
$$

### Solution

$$
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$

## Cubic Equation

$$
y = ax^3 + bx^2 + cx + d
$$

### Solution

Let

$$
\Delta _{0}=b^{2}-3ac\\
\Delta _{1}=2b^{3}-9abc+27a^{2}d
$$

$$
C={\sqrt[{3}]{\frac {\Delta _{1}\pm {\sqrt {\Delta _{1}^{2}-4\Delta _{0}^{3}}}}{2}}}
$$

$$
x_{k}=-{\frac {1}{3a}}\left(b+\xi ^{k}C+{\frac {\Delta _{0}}{\xi ^{k}C}}\right),\qquad k\in \{0,1,2\}
$$

where 

$$
\xi = \frac{-1 + \sqrt{-3}}{2} 
$$

## Logarithms

- $\log_b c = a \implies b^a = c, b \not= 0$

### Properties

- $\log m^n = n \log m$
- $\log (mn) = \log m  + \log n$
- $\log \frac{m}{n} = \log m - \log n$
- $\log_a 1 = 0$
- $\log_a a = 1$
- $\log_{a^b} m = \frac{1}{b}\log_a m$
- $\log_{a^b} m^n = \frac{n}{b}\log_a m$
- $\log_a b = \frac{\log b}{\log a}$
- $\log_a b = \frac{1}{\log_b a}$
- $a^{\log_a x} = x$

# Factorization

- $a^2 - b^2 = (a-b)(a+b)$
- $a^3 - b^3 = (a-b)(a^2 + ab -b^2)$
- $a^3 + b^3 = (a+b)(a^2 - ab -b^2)$
